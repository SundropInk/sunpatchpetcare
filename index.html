A little about me

Trusted care, treated like my own

Hi, I'm Athena Engberg — a local pet sitter based in the Bradburn neighborhood, serving Broomfield (including Anthem Ranch and Via Varra) as well as Westminster, Northglenn, Thornton, and Arvada, offering dependable, hands-on care for dogs and cats: walks, drop-ins, and overnight stays.

I've been working in professional pet care since September 2024 alongside VIPet Sitter Service, and launched Sun Patch Pet Care to bring that same trained, insured care to more of the North Metro on my own.

Every visit comes with clear communication, careful attention to your pet's routine, and the same respect for your home that I'd want for mine.
